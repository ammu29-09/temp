Starbucks

Founded in 1971, Starbucks is a global coffeehouse chain headquartered in Seattle, Washington. Known for its premium coffee drinks, it also offers pastries, snacks, and teas. Starbucks has expanded worldwide with thousands of locations, becoming a symbol of coffee culture. The company places a strong emphasis on sustainability, ethical sourcing, and community engagement.

Amazon

Established in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon started as an online bookstore and quickly transformed into a giant e-commerce platform. It offers a wide range of products, from electronics to groceries, and has expanded into services like Amazon Web Services (AWS), Amazon Prime, and streaming content. Amazon is one of the largest tech companies globally, with a major influence on retail, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence.
